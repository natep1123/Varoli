# 00 — Overview: What Diving Is

*This folder contains the full mechanics of diving: the practice, the domains, the compression, the crossing, and the poem. If Yemu is the language, diving is what you do with it.*

---

## The Origin

The dive poem came first.

Before the phonemic schema was complete. Before most of the consonants had names. Before the structural rules were formalized. There was a structure for a descent that became something else entirely before it came back up. The language was built around it. The grammar was developed to explain it. The terminology was locked to protect it.

Everything in this folder exists because that poem exists.

---

## The World

In Recurra, the ocean is everything.

Islands rise and drown. The Witnesses who live on them are nomadic by necessity: all islands drown eventually. They move constantly, across water they have not crossed before, toward islands they have never seen. The ocean is not their obstacle. It is their home.

At a certain depth the light disappears and the water changes character. What was relational and flowing becomes something heavier, darker, more interior. Most Witnesses experience this as the limit of the body: the point where breath runs out and the depth pulls without asking. This is the threshold of the Black Sun.

The Journeymen experience it differently.

They are the nine fully actualized Witnesses of the 33rd dimension, the highest layer of awareness available on the surface. They have learned something no other being in their world knows: the structure of what is below. For them the disappearance of light is not a limit. It is a signal. The darkness is not an absence. It is a different kind of presence. The deep is navigable. The Black Sun is not the end. It is the medium.

The ocean is the world. The dive is the practice. What you are actually doing when you dive is moving through the ontological domains that make up the architecture of existence itself. The water is the medium. The depth is the direction.

---

## What the Journeymen Know

The Journeymen are the keepers of this practice. They know Yemu. They know the structure of the dive. They know what each domain requires and what it costs to go deeper than the previous dive took them.

This is not metaphorical knowledge. It is operational. The Journeymen use Yemu as a navigational instrument, not to describe the dive from the outside but to move through it from the inside. The gate codes are not spoken aloud. They are registered within the self. Declarations to the self. The diver knows where they are because the structure tells them, and the structure holds them in coherence while everything else dissolves.

What the Journeymen seek varies by character. The methodology is the same regardless of the intention. The structure of the dive does not care what you are looking for. It only cares that you know how to carry yourself through it and back.

The dive poem is their map. Yemu is the only language precise enough to carry the retrieval back intact.

---

## The Metaphysical Dimension

This is a metaphysical experience without substances.

The compression mechanics, the gate codes, the zone declarations, the Y crossing: these are a technology. A way of moving awareness through states that most traditions require years of dedicated practice or specific substances to access. The language provides the structure that makes the movement deliberate rather than chaotic.

The implications for someone who knows this language and encounters these states through any other means are not mapped. What can be said is that the language was not built around any substance. It was built around the structure of the experience itself. Wherever that structure appears, through practice, through dream, through any other means, the language is there to receive it and to carry what was found back across the threshold intact.
