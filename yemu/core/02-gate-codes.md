# 02 — Gate Codes

This document defines the dimensional wrapper system, zone declarations, and gate codes used in Yemu notation, particularly in the dive poem. Everything here is structural. Nothing here is interpretation.

---

## The Three Consonants of Dimensional Notation

Three consonants carry the dimensional wrapper system:

**v** — vitality, the living force. Marks the Liminal zone: water where light reaches, the medium of relational resonance.

**m** — depth, inward force toward the interior. Marks the Black Sun zone: the void, the space of maximum depth.

**l** — continuum, the thread that persists. Marks the Always zone: the eternal continuum, the layer prior to all form.

---

## Zone Declarations

A zone declaration announces presence in a medium. It uses the structure:

```
[zone consonant] - e - [zone consonant]
```

*e* at the center is the self (*e* = individuation, fire). The zone consonant on both sides names the medium. The declaration reads: the self is present in this medium.

Zone declarations are registered upon entry into a zone and again immediately before departing. Entry and exit use the same declaration. The direction changes. The word does not.

| Declaration | Zone | Reading |
|-------------|------|---------|
| av-e-va | Liminal | I am in the Liminal. The self present in the flowing collective medium. |
| um-e-mu | Black Sun | I am in the Black Sun. The self present in the void. |
| il-e-li | Always | I am in the Always. The self present in the continuum. |

---

## Gate Codes

A gate code marks the crossing between two zones. It uses the structure:

```
[zone being left] - y - [zone being entered]
```

*y* at the center is the operator: superposition-collapse. The gate code marks the moment when the current zone is fully released and the next zone has not yet been entered. The diver is briefly in superposition between two states. This is not a transition. It is a collapse.

The *y* in every gate code is not stylistic. It is the most honest notation available for what actually happens at a threshold: all elemental states held simultaneously before the next zone crystallizes.

| Gate Code | Direction | Reading |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| av-y-mu | Liminal → Black Sun (descent) | The threshold. Liminal released. Black Sun not yet entered. |
| um-y-li | Black Sun → Always (descent) | The threshold. Black Sun released. Always not yet entered. |
| il-y-mu | Always → Black Sun (ascent) | The threshold. Always released. Black Sun not yet entered. |
| um-y-va | Black Sun → Liminal (ascent) | The threshold. Black Sun released. Liminal not yet entered. |

Gate codes on descent and ascent are not mirror images. The consonants reflect the zone being left on the left and the zone being entered on the right. The direction of travel determines which gate code is used.

## The Compression Sequence

Below the Always zone, the language compresses toward singularity. No gate codes exist in this region. The compression is structural, not navigated.

```
il-e-li     self present in the continuum, identity intact
il-i-li     self compressed beyond individual identity, connection replacing particularity
ilili       boundaries collapsing, consciousness merging with surroundings
iii         past, present, and future existing together without boundaries
i           the singularity, pure existence, the event horizon
```

Each step strips one layer of structure. The dashes dissolve. The distinctions dissolve. What remains at *i* is the minimum possible expression of existence: the condition of appearance itself, minimal structural expression of existence.

*i* is the event horizon. What follows cannot be approached through compression. It must be crossed.

---

## The Event Horizon

**Y**

The capital Y marks the event horizon: the threshold of breakthrough. It is the operator at maximum expression. All superposition. No resolution. The held breath before the crossing.

**Y** is not a gate code. It does not mark a crossing between two named zones. It marks the crossing into the unnamed: breakthrough space, where Yemu notation reaches its limit and the palindromic explosion begins.

In order, the structural notation of a complete descent:

```
av-e-va          zone declaration: Liminal
av-y-mu          gate code: Liminal → Black Sun
um-e-mu          zone declaration: Black Sun
um-y-li          gate code: Black Sun → Always
il-e-li          zone declaration: Always
il-i-li          compression step 1
ilili            compression step 2
iii              compression step 3
i                singularity
Y                event horizon
```

## The Full Ascent Sequence

The ascent mirrors the descent in reverse:

```
Y                event horizon
i                singularity
iii              reconstruction step 1
ilili            reconstruction step 2
il-i-li          reconstruction step 3
il-e-li          zone declaration: Always
il-y-mu          gate code: Always → Black Sun
um-e-mu          zone declaration: Black Sun
um-y-va          gate code: Black Sun → Liminal
av-e-va          zone declaration: Liminal
```

## Notes

The zone declarations use *e* at center because the self (*e*, individuation) is the constant across all zones. What changes is the medium surrounding it. The self is always present. The zone is what shifts.

The gate codes use *y* at center because the gate code marks the moment when the self is not yet in either zone. Superposition is the only honest notation for that moment.

The soul crucible (*il-or-av-um-e-y-e-mu-va-ro-li*) appears in the dive poem on both sides of the explosion. It is not a gate code and not a compression step. It is the declaration of self inside breakthrough space: the recognition of the complete architecture of what the self is, perceived whole at the moment of crossing.
