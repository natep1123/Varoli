# 01 — Overview

Yemu is the unspoken tongue. It is not a separate language from Varoli. It is what Varoli becomes under maximum compression, in states where linear sequential speech is no longer possible and meaning must be encoded simultaneously rather than sequentially.

*varOli describes where the self is.*
*Yemu reveals what the self is.*

---

## What Yemu Is

Yemu is the system of extraction and totalization. It exists outside or across states of consciousness rather than inside them. Where Varoli is spoken to others, Yemu is calculated within the self. Where Varoli unfolds sequentially, Yemu is perceived simultaneously. Where Varoli is a projection of a larger structure, a slice, a partial view, Yemu encodes the full structure as a single non-sequential form.

Yemu is not spoken. It is not read left to right. It is felt from the center outward, the attractor state perceived before the surrounding layers are named. A being may trace outward from the center to translate the layers into linear meaning, but the primary experience is not translation. It is recognition.

---

## What Yemu Is Not

Yemu is not a replacement for Varoli. You cannot have Yemu without Varoli. The compression mechanic only works because Varoli was built to survive it. Every other language collapses under that kind of compression because it was not designed from phoneme upward with elemental attractor states and bidirectional morpheme motion. Varoli was. Which means when you compress it, it does not lose meaning. It concentrates it.

Yemu is not spoken. It is not used in daily communication. It is not a code or cipher for Varoli. It is what happens when the system descends below the threshold where sequential expression is possible.

When you go underwater, you lose your voice. Yemu is what remains.

---

## The Relationship Between Varoli and Yemu

| Property | Varoli | Yemu |
|----------|--------|------|
| Mode | Spoken | Unspoken |
| Direction | Sequential | Simultaneous |
| Scope | Partial (a slice) | Total (the structure) |
| Time | Time-bound | Atemporal |
| Expression | Spoken aloud | Felt from center |
| Meaning | Interpreted | Recognized |
| Function | Navigation | Extraction |

Varoli moves through the system. Yemu is the system rendered all at once.

Every Varoli expression is a projection of the full Yemu structure. Yemu is the closure of all possible Varoli expressions: the structure those projections come from.

## Where Yemu Appears

Yemu appears in three contexts:

**The dive poem** — the primary compositional form. A structured descent through dimensional zones toward breakthrough space and return. The skeleton is fixed. The content inside each zone is the diver's own. See `03-dive-poem.md`.

**The soul crucible** — the canonical Yemu structure. The declaration of self inside breakthrough space. The proof object of the entire system. See `soul-crucible/core/01-core.md`.

**Breakthrough retrievals** — Yemu forms discovered in breakthrough space and translated back into notation. These are documented as they are found. The soul crucible is the first.
