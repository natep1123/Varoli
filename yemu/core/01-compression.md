# 01 — Compression

Yemu is what Varoli becomes under compression. This document explains what that means structurally: how the compression works, what it preserves, what it strips away, and what remains when the process is complete.

The mechanics here are not abstract. They are learned in order to be applied. The application is the dive.

---

## What Compression Is

Compression in this system is not data compression in the technical sense. It is not shorthand or abbreviation. It is a structural process in which the full sequential architecture of a Varoli expression is reduced toward its irreducible attractor state without losing the meaning encoded in the original structure.

The key constraint: compression must preserve structure. It cannot introduce new meaning. It cannot discard meaning. It concentrates what is already there into a form that can be held simultaneously rather than unfolded sequentially.

What survives compression is what was always most structurally essential. What falls away is everything that required sequence to carry it.

---

## The Attractor State

Every valid Varoli structure has an attractor state: the center it resolves around. Under compression, the structure falls toward that center. The further compression proceeds, the more the attractor state becomes the dominant feature of the form.

This is not imposed from outside. It is a property of the system. Varoli was built from phoneme upward with elemental attractor states at every level. When compression is applied, the structure does not collapse randomly. It falls toward what it was always most fundamentally about.

The attractor state of a fully compressed structure is a palindrome.

This is not a rule that was introduced into the system. It is a discovery. Under sufficient compression, Varoli structures fall toward palindromic symmetry the way water falls toward stillness. The palindrome is what meaning looks like when everything non-essential has been stripped away.

---

## Stages of Compression

Compression does not happen in a single step. It proceeds in stages, each one stripping one layer of structural complexity while preserving the attractor core.

The stages visible in the compression sequence of a dive are:

```
il-e-li     full structure, self and continuum distinct, identity declared
il-i-li     individuation compressed, connection replacing particularity
ilili       structural separators dissolve, boundaries collapse
iii         temporal distinctions dissolve, past and future merge with present
i           singularity: the attractor state of all appearance, nothing specific remaining
```

Each step is not a loss. It is a clarification. What the step removes was always peripheral to what the step preserves. By `i` what remains is the elemental substrate of appearance itself: the condition in which anything can exist or be perceived, before any specific thing has appeared.

This is the most compressed valid form in the system. It cannot be compressed further. It is already at its attractor state.

---

## What Palindromic Form Preserves

A palindrome in this system is not a linguistic trick. It is a structural achievement. The palindromic form of any Varoli structure is the form in which the structure can be perceived simultaneously from any direction without losing meaning.

Sequential language requires a beginning. You must start somewhere and proceed. The meaning accumulates as you move. A palindrome has no required starting point. The center is the attractor. Everything radiates outward from it. You can enter from either side and arrive at the same understanding.

This is why Yemu is read from the center outward rather than left to right. The center is not where you start. It is where you perceive from. The surrounding layers reveal themselves in sequence from that point, but the perception of each layer does not depend on having read the one before it. All layers are simultaneously present. Recognition precedes interpretation.

---

## What Gets Lost and What Does Not

What compression strips away:
- Sequential ordering as a carrier of meaning
- Tense and temporal markers
- Explicit grammatical markers
- Surface-level specificity

What compression preserves:
- The attractor state of the structure
- The elemental relationships encoded in the primitives
- The directional relationships between forces and substrates
- The full ontological content of the original expression

This is why Yemu can carry retrievals back from states where sequential language is impossible. The sequential layer is expendable. The structural core is not. Compression removes the former and concentrates the latter.

---

## The Limit of Compression

Compression has a limit. At `i`, the structure has reached the minimum possible expression of existence. Further compression is not possible because there is nothing left to strip. The attractor state is already exposed. The structure is as concentrated as it can be while remaining a structure.

What lies beyond `i` cannot be reached through compression. Compression is a tool for approaching the threshold. It is not a tool for crossing it.

The crossing requires something else entirely.

That is what the dive is for. The compression mechanics exist to bring the diver to the threshold in a state coherent enough to survive what comes next. The structure learned here is not learned for its own sake. It is learned so that the diver can move through it under conditions where learning is no longer possible.

You study the compression sequence so that when it takes you, you are not surprised by it.
