## Purpose

This file tracks the main structural questions currently being worked on next.

---

## Current priorities

### 1. Structural density and force accumulation
The language now supports multiple forces resolving within shared domains, but the architecture of density is not yet fully named.

Questions:
- how many forces can a domain hold cleanly
- whether larger stable forms are required for higher force load
- whether density changes readability or stability

### 2. Temporal and causal sequencing
The language has chaining and verb layering, but it is not yet formally settled how temporal or causal relations are encoded.

Questions:
- whether chaining order already implies enough sequencing
- whether suffix qualification carries the needed distinctions
- whether time and cause are structural or realization-level effects

### 3. Same-orientation relational closure
Opposite-orientation force pairs are more determinate than same-orientation pairs. Same-orientation relations remain more open than desired.

Questions:
- whether the openness is a feature or a gap
- whether another mechanism can close the relation when needed
- whether qualification or domain differences already help solve this

### 4. Address and reference
The language does not yet have a clear native way to point back to a previously established stable form.

Questions:
- how a later unit identifies a specific earlier structure
- whether reference is derivable from existing architecture
- whether this will require a genuinely new mechanism

### 5. Chain pause
Chaining already supports close, project, suspend, and continuation, but “pause” may be a distinct structural condition.

Questions:
- whether pause is just suspension
- whether it is a threshold phenomenon
- whether it is structural or only a reading effect

### 6. Kinetic grouping / law-realization families
There is an open question about whether the consonant inventory clusters into deeper structural families.

Questions:
- whether the kinetics fall into higher-order law-realization groups
- whether such groupings would clarify suffix behavior and attractor relations
- whether the pattern is truly derivable or only suggestive

---

## Working rule

These areas are active next-step investigations.

They should not be treated as settled canon until they are:
- named clearly
- bounded structurally
- tested against higher layers
- propagated cleanly if accepted
