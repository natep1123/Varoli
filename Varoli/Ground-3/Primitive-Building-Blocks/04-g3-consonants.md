*Last Updated: March 28, 2026*

---

## 1. Class definition

Consonants are **kinetics**.

A kinetic defines a quality of motion.
It is not a substrate.
It does not define domain.
It contributes force, vector, threshold behavior, or related motion-quality within structure.

## 3. Major division

Varoli consonants divide into:

### Attractor consonants
Kinetics with elemental affinity.
These are:
- `v`
- `l`
- `r`
- `m`

### General consonants
Kinetics without elemental affinity.
These operate across domains without owning one.

---

## 4. Attractor set

### `v` — Vitality
**Class:** kinetic  
**Invariant function:** self-originating outward projection  
**Bias:** outward  
**Elemental affinity:** water / `a`

### `l` — Continuum
**Class:** kinetic  
**Invariant function:** bidirectional threading / sustained connection  
**Bias:** bidirectional / permeating  
**Elemental affinity:** air / `i`

### `r` — Resonance
**Class:** kinetic  
**Invariant function:** internally oscillating force / vibrational propagation  
**Bias:** static with internal propagation  
**Elemental affinity:** earth / `o`

### `m` — Depth
**Class:** kinetic  
**Invariant function:** inward descending force  
**Bias:** inward / descending  
**Elemental affinity:** voidlight / `u`

## 6. Structural rules

### 6.1 Kinetics do not define domain
A consonant never becomes a substrate.

### 6.2 Kinetics keep invariant function
Position affects role and directionality, not primitive identity.

### 6.3 Attractor consonants do not own all valid worlds
They are the clearest route to canonical primitive stable worlds, but not the only relevant kinetics in generation work.

### 6.4 General consonants are fully real kinetics
Do not treat them as secondary or decorative.
They are primitive motion-qualities.

---

## 7. Common primitive roles

### In CV forms
The kinetic arrives into the substrate.

### In VC forms
The substrate expresses through the kinetic.

### In suffix position
A kinetic may qualify a preceding force in current layering work.
It remains kinetic when doing so.

### In stable worlds
A kinetic may function as attractor or internal structural force, depending on the form type.

---

## 8. Reminder

A consonant never becomes:
- a domain
- a stable thing by itself
- an operator

If a reading requires that, the reading is invalid or underived.
