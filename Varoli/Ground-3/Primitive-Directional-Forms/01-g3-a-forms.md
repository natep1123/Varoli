## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- **CV** forms describe force arriving into relation
- **VC** forms describe relation expressing through force

Examples:
- `va` = vitality entering relation
- `av` = relation expressing through vitality
- `sa` = seeking entering relation
- `az` = relation expressing through recognition

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive directional forms only.
They are not stable worlds and not layered forms.
