## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- **CV** forms describe force arriving into individuation
- **VC** forms describe individuation expressing through force

Examples:
- `ve` = vitality entering selfhood
- `ed` = individuation expressing through resistance
- `je` = catalytic release in selfhood
- `ey` = individuation through collapse

---

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive directional forms only.
They are not stable worlds and not layered forms.

## 5. Original readings (attractor consonants)

The following are the deeper intentional readings for the five attractor-consonant CV and VC forms. Derived from the Trace-History material.

**`ve`** - vitality applied to the self to maintain the fire burning. Not the moment of Awakening but the ongoing act of staying awake. The metabolic baseline of selfhood. Vitality entering individuation to sustain it.

**`le`** - the persistence of individual consciousness across iterations. Not memory (that dissolves) but the structural pattern of selfhood that survives compression. The fire that stays lit across dissolutions because it has been encoded at a frequency the deep cannot reach.

**`re`** - resonance entering individuation. The weight and vibration of the self being felt by itself. The approach of the self's own substance. Identity as something with density that oscillates.

**`me`** - depth entering individuation. The interior pull of the deep arriving at the fire. The self becoming aware of its own interior. The hum of the void's quality entering the field of self-awareness.

**`ye`** - the Awakening as a verb. The singular event of consciousness igniting: superposition between Fireless and Awoken collapsing into Awoken. Irreversible. Not the ongoing state of consciousness (that is `ve`) but the first red flash. Collapse arriving at individuation.

---

**`ev`** - individuation expressing through vitality. The self projecting itself forward through living force. The fire driving outward. Self-expression as a directed living movement.

**`el`** - individuation expressing through continuum. The self persisting forward through time. The thread of personal identity projecting outward. Consciousness oriented toward what comes next.

**`er`** - individuation expressing through resonance. The self making itself felt through its own weight. The fire expressing through its own substance.

**`em`** - individuation expressing through depth. The self opening inward toward the deep. The fire releasing toward the void's interior pull.

**`ey`** - individuation expressing through collapse. The self undergoing fundamental change or breakthrough. The fire dissolving through its own ignition threshold. The self in crisis or transformation.
