- **CV** forms describe force arriving into appearance
- **VC** forms describe appearance expressing through force

Examples:
- `vi` = vitality entering presence
- `li` = continuum entering field
- `zi` = recognition in presence
- `ith` = appearance through dissolution

## 5. Original readings (attractor consonants)

The following are the deeper intentional readings for the five attractor-consonant CV and VC forms. Derived from the Trace-History material.

**`vi`** - vitality in the universal medium. The baseline frequency of existence. The active maintenance of being through breath. Not just "being" but the ongoing living pulse within the condition of all appearance.

**`li`** - the suffix `-li` as a standalone verb: forward, becoming, the direction of the exhale. Not future tense (that is grammatical marking) but the ontological state of being oriented toward what has not yet arrived. The Walk expressed as a single syllable.

**`ri`** - resonance entering appearance. The weight and vibration of things making themselves felt within the universal field. The approach of substantial things within the medium that holds everything.

**`mi`** - depth entering appearance. The interior pull of the deep arriving at the condition of all appearance. The void's quality entering the universal medium. The hum of immense things within the field of existence.

**`yi`** - all possible connections collapsing into the specific connections that actually exist. Universal superposition resolving. The mechanism that makes the field selective rather than total. Everything could be connected to everything. `yi` is the event that makes only the relevant connections real.

---

**`iv`** - appearance expressing through vitality. The universal field projecting itself through living force. Existence itself in motion. The condition of all appearance driving forward.

**`il`** - appearance expressing through continuum. The universal field threading forward. The breath that does not stop. The condition of appearance persisting through time as a continuous structure.

**`ir`** - appearance expressing through resonance. The universal field making itself felt through its own weight. The medium of all appearance resonating.

**`im`** - appearance expressing through depth. The universal field opening toward the interior pull. The condition of appearance releasing toward the void.

**`iy`** - appearance expressing through collapse. The universal field itself undergoing a state change. The condition of all appearance passing through a threshold event. The medium-as-such resolving.
