## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- **CV** forms describe force arriving into origin
- **VC** forms describe origin expressing through force

Examples:
- `mu` = depth in origin
- `thu` = dissolution in origin
- `zu` = recognition in origin
- `uy` = origin through collapse

---

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive directional forms only.
They are not stable worlds and not layered forms.

## 5. Original readings (attractor consonants)

The following are the deeper intentional readings for the five attractor-consonant CV and VC forms. Derived from the Trace-History material.

**`vu`** - living force entering the between-state. The verb of intentional dissolution: vitality directed not toward form or the collective but toward the threshold itself. The conscious dive made into a single word. Not dying. Not surrendering. Choosing the void. The Journeymen's characteristic action: bringing vitality deliberately into the voidlight medium.

**`lu`** - the thread that continues even through dissolution. The persistence mechanism that the deep cannot cut. Not the content of what persists (that varies) but the structural continuity of the thread itself within the between-state. Even in dissolution the thread of `lu` continues.

**`ru`** - resonance entering undifferentiated origin. The weight and vibration of substantial things arriving at the between-state. The approach of something dense toward the threshold.

**`mu`** - depth arriving at undifferentiated origin. The interior resonance of the deep arriving at the void itself. Depth entering its own medium. The Black Sun's attractor force in its home domain.

**`yu`** - collapse arriving at the between-state rather than at a resolved actuality. The output type is `u`: another threshold, another undeclared state. The most difficult CV to process because the collapse produces further unresolvedness rather than resolving it. The moment a person fires `yu` they have collapsed into a between-state where neither their surface life nor the deep has them fully.

---

**`uv`** - origin expressing through vitality. The between-state projecting its living force outward. The void in motion. The threshold itself driving forward.

**`ul`** - origin expressing through continuum. The between-state threading forward. The dissolution that does not end continuity.

**`ur`** - origin expressing through resonance. The void expressing its weight outward. The between-state making itself felt through its own frequency. The hum of the deep arriving at the surface.

**`um`** - origin expressing through depth. The void expressing itself through its own interior resonance. The Black Sun's attractor pair: `u` departing through `m`. This is the departure face of the Black Sun's canonical stable world.

**`uy`** - the between-state collapsing further. The threshold undergoing its own resolution event. Recursion of dissolution: the void in which collapse acts is itself becoming the subject of collapse. The between-one becoming aware of itself as between.
