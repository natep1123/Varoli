*Last Updated: March 28, 2026*

**Status:** Working reference

**Authority:** Primitive stable-world inventory  
**Scope:** Lists all same-envelope primitive stable worlds balanced equally by `a` (relational resonance).

---

## 1. Envelope anchor

`a` = relational resonance  
The medium through which distinct things affect, connect, and dissolve into one another.

These worlds are built from:
- relation on the left
- centered force or operator
- relation on the right

## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- the outer substrate is always `a`
- the center changes
- the stable world changes according to the centered primitive

Examples:
- `aVa` = relation alive with vitality
- `aDa` = relation held in resistance
- `aSa` = relation organized around seeking

---

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive same-envelope stable worlds only.
They are not directional forms, expanded stable worlds, or cross-envelope worlds.
