**Authority:** Primitive stable-world inventory  
**Scope:** Lists all same-envelope primitive stable worlds balanced equally by `u` (undifferentiated origin).

---

## 1. Envelope anchor

`u` = undifferentiated origin  
The substrate prior to distinction, relation, form, individuation, and field.

These worlds are built from:
- origin on the left
- centered force or operator
- origin on the right

## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- the outer substrate is always `u`
- the center changes
- the stable world changes according to the centered primitive

Examples:
- `uMu` = canonical deep origin-world
- `uLu` = origin organized around enduring continuum
- `uPu` = origin organized around compression

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive same-envelope stable worlds only.
They are not directional forms, expanded stable worlds, or cross-envelope worlds.
