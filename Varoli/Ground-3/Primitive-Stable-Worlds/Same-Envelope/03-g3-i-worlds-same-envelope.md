## 1. Envelope anchor

`i` = condition of appearance  
The condition in which anything may appear or be perceived.

These worlds are built from:
- appearance on the left
- centered force or operator
- appearance on the right

## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- the outer substrate is always `i`
- the center changes
- the stable world changes according to the centered primitive

Examples:
- `iLi` = canonical enduring field-world
- `iZi` = presence organized around recognition
- `iPi` = field organized around compression

---

## 4. Reminder

These are primitive same-envelope stable worlds only.
They are not directional forms, expanded stable worlds, or cross-envelope worlds.
