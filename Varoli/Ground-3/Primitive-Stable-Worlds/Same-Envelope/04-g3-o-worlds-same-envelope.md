## 3. Quick read

In this file:
- the outer substrate is always `o`
- the center changes
- the stable world changes according to the centered primitive

Examples:
- `oRo` = canonical resonant form-world
- `oDo` = form organized around resistance
- `oZo` = form organized around recognition
