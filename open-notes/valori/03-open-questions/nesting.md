# Nesting (Open Question)

## Problem

How to represent hierarchical relationships without introducing:
- parentheses
- grouping syntax
- new grammar rules

---

## Example Need

“I as friend within island”

Currently:

```

e ve oRro va arOva

```

Works linearly.

---

## Potential Need

More complex structures may require:
- grouping
- scope
- precedence

---

## Risk

Nesting introduces:
- non-linear interpretation rules
- potential ambiguity

---

## Constraint

Any nesting solution must:
- be derivable
- not introduce symbolic grouping

---

## Open Questions

- Can structure alone express hierarchy?
- Does ordering become necessary at higher complexity?
